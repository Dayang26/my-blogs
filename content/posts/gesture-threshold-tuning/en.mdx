---
title: Tuning Gesture Detection Thresholds
excerpt: Lighting and background shifts can heavily affect recognition accuracy.
---

## Setup

- Indoor lighting 200–400 lux
- Dark vs. bright background
- Sampling at 30Hz

## Findings

- Low light requires a +0.05 to +0.1 threshold bump.
- Too high a threshold drops light pinch gestures.

## Recommendation

Stabilize detection first, then gradually increase sensitivity.
