excerpt: The keys to staying above 30 FPS are sampling cadence, filtering strategy, and render isolation.
---

## Goal

Keep interaction stable under low light and complex backgrounds while sustaining 30+ FPS.

## Key Strategies

- Reduce per-frame work by moving heavy tasks into a fixed sampling loop.
- Use a threshold gate before smoothing to avoid overfitting noise.
- Separate rendering from detection so the renderer only consumes stable states.

## Results

On the same hardware, frame jitter dropped from 12 FPS to 3 FPS and false positives reduced.

## Summary

Performance tuning is about predictability and headroom, not cutting features.
