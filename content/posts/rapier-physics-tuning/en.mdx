## Key Parameters

- Friction: controls sliding and settling
- Mass: defines inertia and responsiveness
- Impulse threshold: controls throw amplitude

## Tip

Use "mass tiers" so heavier items feel slower and steadier.

## Example

```
const impulse = Math.min(pinchStrength * 1.4, 1.0);
```
